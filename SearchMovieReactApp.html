Search Movie App

The movie searching app, developed with React.js and powered by the OMDb API, allows users to search for movies based on provided names and retrieves detailed information about the matching movies. With a user-friendly interface, the app seamlessly displays the movie titles, release years, and other relevant details, providing users with a concise overview of the movies they are interested in. Experience the convenience and efficiency of this React-based movie searching app as you explore and discover a wide range of movies.

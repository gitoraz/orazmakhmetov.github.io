E-Commerce Website

TechShop, an immersive E-commerce website specializing in electronics and accessories, offers a wide range of functionalities to enhance the shopping experience. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this dynamic platform incorporates features such as signup and login pages, a logout feature, product adding capabilities, and a dedicated seller's dashboard.

Click here to access the website

The project is currently in development and undergoing continuous updates to ensure its optimal functionality. Stay tuned for future updates.

The signup and login pages provide users with a seamless onboarding experience, allowing them to create personalized accounts and securely access their profiles. This feature enables customers to track orders, manage their preferences, and benefit from personalized recommendations.

The website also includes a product adding feature, allowing sellers to easily add and manage their products. This functionality streamlines the process of showcasing new items, updating existing inventory, and maintaining accurate product information.
